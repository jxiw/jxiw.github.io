Junxiong Wang

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell University, where I worked at the intersection of large language models and systems, with a focus on linear models and their hybrid variants. I lead multiple research projects at Together AI, including adaptive speculative decoding (ATLAS), inference-time training (Aurora), and efficient RL rollouts.
